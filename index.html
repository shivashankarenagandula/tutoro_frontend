Questions

Before you apply

Is there a fee to register? +

No. Requesting a demo class as a parent is free, and creating a tutor profile is free. Tutoro doesn't charge either side to get matched.

Do I need to visit an office? +

No — there's no physical institute. Everything happens over phone and WhatsApp, and classes happen at your home.

How fast will I hear back? +

Most requests are matched and contacted within 24 hours. As we're personally onboarding tutors right now, response can be even faster.

Which areas do you cover? +

We're actively expanding across Hyderabad, prioritising areas with both an active parent request and a matching tutor nearby.
